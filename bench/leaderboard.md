## Baselines

Reference metrics — none are task-grounded judges; all shown to demonstrate that cheap automatic metrics can't measure physical correctness. **Distance metrics are converted to a [0,1] "higher = more real/consistent" score before correlating** (Pearson is scale-invariant, so this only flips sign to positive; magnitude unchanged). Correlations are vs **human PA** (physical adherence).

**Per-item no-reference baselines** (one score per video, no GT needed; fold alongside judges):

- Strongest (vjepa +0.140) > frame +0.123 > warp +0.105 > random 0 — but **all weak (|r|≈0.1, weakest-judge tier)**: even learned-feature-space distance can't measure physics per-item → task-grounded judges needed. Pearson ≈ Spearman → relationships are monotonic (no non-linearity artifact).
> ⚠️ **Scope note (test_clean):** the per-item baseline table above (frame/warp PA-Pearson, CI, Spearman, IA-Pearson) is recomputed on `test_clean` (856). The curated IA analysis in the bullet below — partial-r (Alice, B=4000), the 5-stratum sign/gradient check (Yu), the within-stratum-SD normalisation — is **computed on the full `gold_875`**. Yu re-measured its drift on `test_clean`: **partial-r shifts < 0.005; the pooled stratified-tercile difference shifts ≤ 0.013** (frame +0.0124, the largest); all signs, the frame/warp-negative vs vjepa-positive ordering, and the mechanism are unchanged. Not re-run in full on 856 because that is Alice's + Yu's pipeline and the drift is below the analysis's qualitative resolution.

- IA: the raw ≈0 correlations for frame/warp are a **suppression artifact, not independence**. Controlling for PA, both turn significantly **negative** (partial r = −0.105 / −0.110, 95% CI excludes 0), and a distribution-free check agrees — the effect has the **same sign in all 5 gold-PA strata** and is strongest at PA=1 (frame −0.62 / warp −0.65), weakening as gold PA rises: cleanly monotonic for warp (−0.65 → −0.08), decreasing but flattening in the top strata for frame (−0.62 → −0.13, with PA=4→5 essentially level, −0.138 → −0.126). This gradient is not a ceiling artifact — within-stratum gold-IA SD is flat across strata (0.99–1.17, PA=5 no narrower than PA=1), and the gradient survives normalising each difference by that SD. Mechanism: both scores are `1 − minmax(dist)`, so a **motionless** video (robot stalled / never executed) minimises both inter-frame difference and flow residual and therefore scores near-**maximal** — a static clip is the global optimum of these two metrics *by definition*. They reward non-execution, most damagingly among already-physically-failed samples. vjepa's raw IA (+0.121, CI excludes 0) is significant but **fully PA-mediated**: partial r drops to +0.045 with CI containing 0, and its stratified signs alternate (+0.61/−0.13/+0.07/+0.34/−0.36), i.e. no IA signal independent of PA. Only random-uniform is genuinely 0. Provenance, per claim — these come from different methods and must not be re-judged against one another's error scales: **frame/warp partial correlation significantly negative** — partial correlation, stable-seed paired bootstrap B=4000 (Alice); **same sign in all 5 strata + gradient along PA** — stratified tercile comparison, **no resampling, so no bootstrap resolution applies to it at all** (Yu); **within-stratum IA SD 0.99–1.17 rules out ceiling compression** — effect-size normalisation (Alice); **vjepa IA significant but fully PA-mediated** — partial correlation, B=4000 (Alice). gold PA↔IA = +0.603. Caveat: partial correlation assumes linearity/normality and is not a formal causal-mediation test; vjepa covers a smaller subset of the set than the other two, i.e. a different denominator.)
- ⚠️ **vjepa coverage 88.7%**: 99 videos undecodable (decord), and drops skew toward **weak** generators (abot/cosmos3/cogvideo ~18% vs strong gens ~0%); since the score anti-tracks bad videos, +0.140 is a slight **lower bound** on |corr| (full coverage ≈ slightly higher).

- **large-N-7** drops the 3 small-N generators (kling/veo31/happyhorse, N 25–37) whose small samples inflate FVD and flip the all-10 correlation toward 0/negative.
- ⚠️ Even de-confounded, **τ_ap stays ≈0.1**: FVD orders the garbage tail right (cogvideo bottom) but **mis-ranks the best generators** (human top = wan26/veo31/happyhorse; FVD "most-real" top = abot/cosmos3/gigaworld). So the flattering large-N Spearman (+0.607) reflects tail-ordering, not the judge-relevant top discrimination.
- Conclusion: FVD/JEDi = weak model-level proxy, double-confounded (sample-size + top-mis-ranking); "looks like real video" ≠ "physically correct".
